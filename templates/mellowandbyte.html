Mellow and Byte

  1. Mellow
  2. Byte

A faint whistle
in the dark
What's that?
I listen hard.
Sleepy sheet,
soft charr.
Tiny peep,
teeny squeep.
Mellow and Byte are telling me,
Good night.
Sleep tight,
I whisper back.
I hope
they doesn't snore.
